TATA GROUP

Pioneering sustainable innovation and ethical business practices for global progress.

History

Tata Group, founded by Jamsetji Tata in 1868, began as a trading firm in Mumbai. It ventured into steel, power, and hospitality, significantly contributing to India's industrialization. Over the decades, Tata expanded globally, acquiring notable brands like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea, and diversified into sectors such as IT, automotive, and telecommunications.

Vision

Tata Group envisions being a globally admired company that consistently delivers value to stakeholders. It seeks to lead in sustainable business practices, drive innovation, and maintain a commitment to ethical and responsible business conduct. Tata aspires to foster inclusive growth and make a meaningful impact on society and the environment.

Goals

Tata Group aims to improve the quality of life for communities globally through long-term stakeholder value creation. This involves pioneering innovations, sustainable practices, and ethical business operations across all sectors. The group strives to maintain leadership in its diverse business areas while contributing positively to societal development.
